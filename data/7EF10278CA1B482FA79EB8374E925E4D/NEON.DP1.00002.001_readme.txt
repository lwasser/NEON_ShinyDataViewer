Data Product Description: Air temperature, available as one- and thirty-minute averages of 1 Hz observations.  Observations are made by sensors located at multiple heights on the tower infrastructure and by sensors located on the aquatic meteorological station.Temperature observations are made using platinum resistance thermometers, which are housed in a fan aspirated shield to reduce radiative bias.
